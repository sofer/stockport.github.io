Local Support Services

There are a number of local support services in Stockport that can help you.

Samaritans

Confidential listening service for people 24 hours a day, 365 ays a year. Talk to them anytime you like, in your own way, and off the record - about whatever's getting to you.

Call 0161 432 1221 or 084557909090 or email jo@samaritans.org

Beacon Counselling

Confidential counselling service for people over 18 covering depression, anxiety, stress, bereavement and loss, relationships, family breakdown, trauma, childhood abus, domestic violence and abuse.

Call for appointments 0161 440 0055 or email office@beacon-counselling.org.uk
